# Figure 7

## Title
Carrier Verification Workflow

## Drawing Scope
Carrier credential verification, monitoring, and risk scoring workflow.

## Caption
Figure 7 is a workflow diagram showing carrier intake, collection of carrier credentials, verification against one or more internal or external data sources, storage of verification status, and generation of a carrier risk score. In some embodiments, the workflow further includes periodic or event-triggered re-checks, insurance expiration monitoring, and alerts when a carrier no longer satisfies operational or compliance requirements.

## Elements Shown
- Carrier intake
- Credential capture
- External verification
- Verification status store
- Risk score calculation
- Insurance monitoring
- Re-check scheduling
- Alert output

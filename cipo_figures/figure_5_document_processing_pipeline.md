# Figure 5

## Title
Document Processing Pipeline

## Drawing Scope
OCR-based extraction, classification, validation, and expiration management for transport documents.

## Caption
Figure 5 illustrates a document processing pipeline comprising document intake, OCR-based text extraction, document classification, rule or compliance validation, and expiration tracking. The pipeline may be applied to bills of lading, customs forms, insurance certificates, licenses, and related transport records. In some embodiments, extracted data is stored in structured form and used to generate alerts or operational restrictions when required credentials approach expiration or fail validation.

## Elements Shown
- Document intake
- OCR extraction
- Structured field extraction
- Document classification
- Compliance validation
- Expiration tracking
- Alert generation

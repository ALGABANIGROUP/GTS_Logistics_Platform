# Figure 6

## Title
Predictive Maintenance System Diagram

## Drawing Scope
Telemetry collection and predictive maintenance decision flow.

## Caption
Figure 6 depicts a predictive maintenance system comprising telemetry ingestion from vehicles or operational systems, risk or failure prediction logic, maintenance recommendation generation, and related performance outputs such as fuel-efficiency analysis or driver-behavior evaluation. In some embodiments, diagnostic data and historical maintenance results are used to prioritize service actions and reduce unplanned downtime.

## Elements Shown
- Telemetry ingestion
- Diagnostic inputs
- Risk prediction engine
- Maintenance recommendations
- Fuel-efficiency analysis
- Driver behavior analysis
- Maintenance action output

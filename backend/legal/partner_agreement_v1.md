# GTS – ELECTRONIC PARTNER AGREEMENT

## Version 1.0 – Effective Upon E-Signature

---

## 1. Introduction

This Electronic Partner Agreement ("Agreement") governs the collaboration between **GTS** ("Platform Provider") and the **Partner** ("Commercial Partner").

By signing electronically, the Partner acknowledges full acceptance of all terms herein and confirms that they have read, understood, and agreed to this Agreement.

---

## 2. Partnership Structure

2.1 The Platform Provider operates and maintains the GTS Logistics Platform, including the core technology, infrastructure, and related digital services.

2.2 The Partner provides commercial capabilities, client acquisition, and operational market reach in one or more territories, sectors, or customer segments.

2.3 The Parties agree to work together on a non-exclusive basis unless otherwise agreed in a separate written addendum.

---

## 3. Responsibilities

### 3.1 GTS Responsibilities

GTS shall:

- Operate, maintain, and secure the platform.
- Provide reasonable technical support for platform usage.
- Maintain commercially reasonable system uptime and availability.
- Manage billing, invoicing, and revenue tracking for platform-based transactions.
- Provide a partner dashboard and related reporting tools.
- Develop ongoing feature updates and improvements on a best-effort basis.

### 3.2 Partner Responsibilities

The Partner shall:

- Acquire and onboard clients to the platform.
- Manage commercial relationships with their own clients.
- Provide Tier-1 support and first-line communication with their clients.
- Ensure accurate and timely data entry into the platform.
- Promote the platform in line with GTS guidelines and branding rules.
- Comply with all applicable laws and regulations in their territory.
- Avoid any misuse of the platform or its data.

---

## 4. Revenue Sharing

### 4.1 General Principles

- Revenue sharing applies to transactions or services that are clearly traceable to the Partner’s activities, clients, or referral codes.
- Net profit or commissionable revenue will be calculated after applicable costs, fees, and discounts.

### 4.2 B2B Services

For Business-to-Business (B2B) services, the default revenue split is:

- **Partner:** 60%  
- **GTS:** 40%

### 4.3 B2C Services

For Business-to-Consumer (B2C) services, the default revenue split is:

- **Partner:** 70%  
- **GTS:** 30%

### 4.4 Marketplace Services

For Marketplace / Carrier / Third-Party services, the default revenue split is:

- **Partner / Carrier:** 80%  
- **GTS:** 20%

### 4.5 Adjustments

- GTS may agree with the Partner on different percentages for specific projects, lanes, or clients, to be documented in writing or in the partner dashboard.
- Any change in revenue model will apply prospectively and will not retroactively affect already-closed periods.

---

## 5. Financial Settlement

5.1 GTS will generate automated monthly revenue reports summarizing:

- Total gross revenue attributable to the Partner.
- Net profit or commissionable revenue.
- Partner share and GTS share.
- Previously paid and pending amounts.

5.2 Payouts to the Partner will generally be processed within **7–14 days** after the close of each settlement period, subject to:

- Successful collection of funds from end clients.
- Completion of any required compliance or verification checks.

5.3 Payments will be made to the Partner’s registered banking or payout method as stored in the partner profile.

5.4 GTS reserves the right to perform audits or reconciliations if there are discrepancies, suspected errors, or suspected misuse.

---

## 6. Data Ownership & Confidentiality

6.1 GTS owns:

- The platform, software, source code, and underlying technical infrastructure.
- All generic system data, analytics, and performance metrics.

6.2 The Partner owns:

- The commercial relationships with their own clients.
- The local business development activities and contacts they generate.

6.3 Both Parties agree to:

- Keep all confidential information received from the other Party secure and protected.
- Use such information only for the purposes of this Agreement.
- Not disclose it to third parties without prior written consent, except as required by law.

---

## 7. Term & Termination

7.1 This Agreement enters into effect upon electronic signature by the Partner.

7.2 The initial term is **24 months**, and it may renew automatically for successive periods unless either Party gives notice of non-renewal.

7.3 Either Party may terminate this Agreement with **30 days’ written notice** for convenience.

7.4 Immediate termination is allowed by GTS in case of:

- Material breach of this Agreement.
- Fraud, abuse, or misuse of the platform.
- Illegal activities, money laundering suspicions, or violations of sanctions or compliance rules.
- Reputational damage to GTS caused by the Partner’s conduct.

7.5 Upon termination:

- Partner access to the platform may be disabled or downgraded.
- Outstanding commissions for already-completed and recognized transactions will still be honored, subject to standard settlement rules.

---

## 8. Liability Limitations

8.1 GTS will use commercially reasonable efforts to keep the platform available and secure but does not guarantee uninterrupted service.

8.2 GTS is not liable for:

- Losses caused by Partner errors, misconfigurations, or inaccurate data entry.
- Third-party failures (carriers, vendors, payment providers, telecom, hosting).
- Force majeure events (including but not limited to natural disasters, war, riots, internet outages, or regulatory shutdowns).

8.3 To the maximum extent permitted by law, the aggregate liability of GTS to the Partner under this Agreement is capped at the total Partner commissions paid during the **last three (3) months** preceding the event giving rise to the claim.

---

## 9. Non-Compete & Non-Solicitation

9.1 During the term of this Agreement and for **12 months** after termination, the Partner shall not:

- Build, operate, or commercially support a platform that directly competes with the core functionality of the GTS Logistics Platform, where such activity is based on confidential information or know-how obtained via this Agreement.

9.2 During the same period, the Partner shall not:

- Intentionally solicit GTS employees, core contractors, or key consultants to leave GTS.
- Intentionally solicit GTS enterprise clients that are not originally the Partner’s own clients for competing services, where such solicitation is based on confidential data from the platform.

---

## 10. Compliance and Acceptable Use

10.1 The Partner agrees to comply with all applicable laws, regulations, trade controls, and sanctions in the jurisdictions where they operate.

10.2 The Partner shall not use the platform for:

- Money laundering or financing illegal activities.
- Fraudulent bookings, fake shipments, or misrepresentation of cargo.
- Any activity that violates international trade laws or customs regulations.

10.3 GTS may temporarily suspend Partner access if there is a reasonable suspicion of non-compliance or misuse, pending investigation.

---

## 11. Amendments & Governing Principles

11.1 GTS may update or amend this Agreement from time to time. Any material changes will be communicated to the Partner, and a new Agreement Version may require renewed acceptance.

11.2 Unless otherwise specified in a local addendum, this Agreement is based on international commercial best practices for logistics, SaaS platforms, and B2B partnerships.

11.3 In case of conflict between this Agreement and a later written addendum signed by both Parties, the addendum prevails.

---

## 🎬 TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

### Video Format
- **Resolution:** 1080p (HD) for desktop
- **Format:** MP4 with H.264 codec
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (24fps for cinematic feel)
- **Aspect Ratios:**
  - YouTube/LinkedIn: 16:9 (1920x1080)
  - Instagram/TikTok: 9:16 (1080x1920) - vertical version
  - Facebook: 1:1 (1080x1080) - square version

### Color Scheme
- **Primary:** Deep Blue (#0E1C2D) - Professional, tech-forward
- **Accent:** Bright Blue (#2289E6) - Energetic, hopeful
- **Success:** Bright Green (#0FBF63) - Achievement, growth
- **Backgrounds:** Dark gradient (#0E1C2D to #1a2f47)
- **Text:** White (#FFFFFF) for contrast

### Animation Style
- **Smooth:** 0.5s ease-in-out transitions
- **Modern:** Floating elements, glow effects
- **Clean:** No clutter, professional appearance
- **Fast-paced:** Keeps viewer engaged

### Music Recommendations
- **Intro (0-3s):** Upbeat tech theme
- **Problem (3-6s):** Slightly tense, minor key
- **Solution (6-10s):** Uplifting modern tech
- **Features (10-22s):** Positive, each bot gets highlight sound
- **Results (22-27s):** Triumphant buildup
- **CTA (27-30s):** Strong, memorable ending

**Royalty-Free Sources:**
- Epidemic Sound
- Artlist.io
- AudioJungle
- Free Music Archive

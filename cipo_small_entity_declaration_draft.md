## Eligibility Reminder
Under the current Canadian patent small entity rules, an eligible entity generally:
- has fewer than 100 employees on the filing date, or is a university; and
- is not controlled directly or indirectly by a non-university entity with 100 or more employees; and
- has not transferred, licensed, or become obligated other than contingently to transfer or license rights in the claimed invention to a non-university entity with 100 or more employees.
